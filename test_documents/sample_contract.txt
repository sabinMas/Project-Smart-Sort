================================================================================
                        SERVICE AGREEMENT
================================================================================

THIS AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is entered into effective as of May 29, 2024
("Effective Date"), by and between:

ACME CORPORATION, a Delaware corporation ("Provider")
AND
SMART INBOX SYSTEMS, INC., a California corporation ("Client")

================================================================================
1. SERVICES
================================================================================

Provider agrees to provide the following services ("Services"):

1.1 Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform for document classification
    and automation
1.2 24/7 technical support and maintenance
1.3 Monthly reporting and analytics
1.4 Regular security updates and patches
1.5 Quarterly business reviews

Services shall commence on June 15, 2024 ("Service Commencement Date").

================================================================================
2. TERM
================================================================================

2.1 Initial Term: One (1) year from the Effective Date
2.2 Renewal: Automatic renewal for successive one-year terms unless either
    party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days prior
    to expiration.

================================================================================
3. COMPENSATION
================================================================================

3.1 Client shall pay Provider a monthly service fee of $2,500.00, due on
    the 1st day of each calendar month.
3.2 Annual amount: $30,000.00
3.3 Payment method: ACH transfer or credit card
3.4 Late payments subject to 1.5% monthly interest

================================================================================
4. CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

4.1 Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's
    proprietary information
4.2 Confidential information shall not be disclosed to third parties without
    prior written consent
4.3 Obligation continues for 2 years after termination

================================================================================
5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
================================================================================

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, OR
CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOST PROFITS, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE
POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES.

TOTAL LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING
THE CLAIM.

================================================================================
6. TERMINATION
================================================================================

6.1 Either party may terminate for material breach if not cured within
    30 days of written notice
6.2 Client may terminate for convenience with 90 days written notice
6.3 Upon termination, Client shall pay all outstanding fees

================================================================================
7. GENERAL PROVISIONS
================================================================================

7.1 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement
7.2 Amendments: May only be amended in writing signed by both parties
7.3 Severability: If any provision is invalid, others remain in effect
7.4 Governing Law: State of California
